Who is Enhyppen?

ENHYPEN is a South Korean boy band formed by Belift Lab, a joint venture between CJ ENM and BigHit Entertainment (now HYBE Corporation).

This group debuted in 2020 after the members were selected through the survival show I-LAND.

They have a strong concept with attractive visuals and diverse personalities, creating unique identity in the world of K-Pop music.

Enhypen Meaning

Enhypen derives its name from the symbol Hyphen (-), that creates new meanings including Connection, Discovery, & Growth

Enhypen aimsto come together to connect, discover and grow together to create a new act.

Enhypen Music

ENHYPEN is known for their diverse musical style, incorporating various genres such as pop, EDM, rock, hip-hop, and R&B, which are dynamically blended to create a fresh vibe in each song.

They often explore complex and deep themes, such as the search for identity, inner conflicts, and the struggle between fate and personal choice.

Their music captures these emotional journeys, offering a unique and immersive experience with each release.
